About Cat Fanciers©

This group was founded on the premise that cats are what own us. We all can agree here that we cannot plan our days without a swift swat of the cat paw to dictate our moves. Events are held semi-annually in a location that is generally TBD. A support section is up-and-coming, for those of you facing cat issues. Chances are that there is a Cat Fancier© member in your area. Admin can help provide contact info for a small fee. Meow.
